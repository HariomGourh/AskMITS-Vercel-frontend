Overview

AskMITS is an AI-powered chatbot explicitly built for MITS students, faculty, and visitors. It acts as a centralized knowledge base, providing instant, accurate answers to common queries ranging from admissions and placements to exams and campus events. Developed by B.Tech CSE students, it aims to accelerate the digitalization of campus services.

How to Use AskMITS

  • Type your specific question into the chat input field at the bottom.
  • Click the Send button or simply press Enter.
  • Allow a few moments for the AI to query the knowledge base and respond.
  • Use the preset Quick Action cards for instant, one-click answers.

Troubleshooting & Feedback

If AskMITS yields an incorrect, outdated, or incomplete response, please report the query via our Contact Page. The chatbot learns strictly from an administered dataset, meaning student feedback directly correlates to future accuracy improvements.
